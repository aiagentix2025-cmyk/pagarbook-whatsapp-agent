VMS Business Details
Company Overview
VINTAQE MANAGEMENT SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED is a technology-driven company providing workforce management solutions for small and medium-sized businesses across India.
The company offers digital solutions that help businesses manage employee attendance, payroll, and field staff operations efficiently.
The flagship product of the company is PagarBook, a staff management and payroll automation platform designed to replace manual registers and spreadsheets with a simple and reliable digital system.
The platform enables business owners to track staff attendance, calculate salaries automatically, monitor field staff activities, and manage workforce operations from a single dashboard.
The system works on mobile applications and web platforms, allowing business owners to manage their staff from anywhere.

Product Overview – PagarBook
PagarBook is a digital employee management system designed mainly for small and medium-sized businesses.
It replaces traditional paper registers and manual salary calculations with an automated system.
Business owners can manage staff attendance, salary calculations, and reports from a single platform.
________________________________________
Main Features
Attendance Management
The system allows staff to mark attendance using:
•	Face recognition attendance
•	Selfie attendance with location
•	Mobile attendance
•	Office attendance
Attendance data is recorded in real-time and stored securely.
________________________________________
Payroll Management
The system automatically calculates salaries based on attendance records.
Features include:
•	Automatic salary calculation
•	Advance and deduction management
•	Payslip generation
•	Attendance reports
This reduces manual calculation errors.
________________________________________
Field Staff Management
The system helps businesses manage field staff effectively.
Features include:
•	Live GPS tracking
•	Staff location monitoring
•	Visit tracking
•	Task assignment
•	Customer management
This ensures staff accountability.
________________________________________
Business Dashboard
Business owners can monitor their staff from a single dashboard.
Dashboard includes:
•	Staff attendance status
•	Salary reports
•	Staff performance
•	Monthly reports
This gives complete business visibility.
________________________________________
Technology Infrastructure
The system is built on cloud infrastructure.
Features include:
•	Secure cloud storage
•	Unlimited data capacity
•	Multi-device access
•	Real-time synchronization
Business owners can access data anytime.
