Staff Management Questions
1. How are you currently managing staff attendance?
Possible answers:
•	Register
•	Excel
•	Biometric
•	WhatsApp
•	No system
This helps understand the current system.
________________________________________
2. How many staff do you currently have?
This helps determine pricing and plan suitability.
________________________________________
3. Do you have office staff, field staff, or both?
This helps identify whether the customer needs:
•	Basic attendance system
•	Payroll system
•	Field tracking system
________________________________________
4. Who calculates salary every month?
Possible answers:
•	Owner
•	Accountant
•	HR
•	Manual calculation
This identifies payroll automation need.
________________________________________
5. How much time does salary calculation take every month?
This helps show time-saving value.
________________________________________
6. Do you ever face attendance or salary disputes with staff?
This identifies business pain.
________________________________________
7. Do you always know where your field staff are during working hours?
Important for Geo plan qualification.
________________________________________
8. Do staff sometimes come late or leave early without proper tracking?
Identifies attendance control issues.
________________________________________
9. Do you maintain attendance records for previous months?
Helps explain reporting benefits.
________________________________________
10. Have you ever faced salary calculation mistakes?
Identifies need for automation.
