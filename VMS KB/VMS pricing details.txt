VMS Pricing Details
Pricing Overview
VINTAQE MANAGEMENT SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED offers flexible pricing based on the number of staff in the business.
The pricing model is designed to be affordable for small and medium-sized businesses while providing complete staff attendance and payroll management automation.
Pricing is calculated per staff per year, which allows businesses to scale the system according to their team size.
All plans include cloud-based access, real-time synchronization, and multi-device support.
GST is applicable as per government regulations.
