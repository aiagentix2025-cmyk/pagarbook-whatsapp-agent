About Your Platform

This platform enables multi-tenant database isolated WhatsApp automation. From the panel on the left, you can manage client configurations.

Each client gets their own database dynamically provisioned inside your PostgreSQL instance, separating their knowledge base document vectors, chat logs, and campaigns.
